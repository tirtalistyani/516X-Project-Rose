Pothole Water Quantity

Introduction

On this page, we will discuss the dynamics of water quantity in potholes. ISU pothole team has been monitoring the pothole water depth from 2016 to 2020. The changes in water depth in a pothole can be affected by several factors, and one of them is precipitation. To understand whether the dynamics of pothole water depth are affected by rainfall, I have downloaded precipitation data of our pothole project area from PRISM Oregon State University.
