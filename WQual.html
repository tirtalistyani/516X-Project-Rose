Pothole Water Quality

Introduction

In this part of the project, I will be focusing on water quality data and try to understand the interaction between each collected parameters. The dynamics of water quality in the potholes can be very complex and depend on many factors in the field. I applied correlation between each parameter to further understand the data. The easiest ways are to visualize it with pairplot and correlation heatmap. Here are the pairplot and heatmap figures per pothole derived from the python seaborn library.
